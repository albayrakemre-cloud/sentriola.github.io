Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. You can delete your account directly from the app or by contacting us.

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed.

Option 1 — Delete from the App

1

Open Sentriola and sign in to your account.

2

Tap the profile icon in the top right corner.

3

Go to Profile.

4

Scroll down and tap Delete Account.

5

Confirm the deletion. Your account and all associated data will be permanently removed.

Option 2 — Request via Email

1

Send an email to support@sentriola.com

2

Use subject: "Account Deletion Request"

3

Include the email address associated with your account.

4

We will process your request within 7 business days.
